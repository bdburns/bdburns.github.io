What is Multipolar?

Multipolar is unconventional band of benevolent geniuses experimenting with radical ideas.

We are a collaborative team of multidiciplinarians who lead innovations in public policy, digital media, emergent technologies, and human-centered civic systems. We also spend quite a bit of time thinking about satellites. Mostly, attack satellites.

We see a future with a seamless boundry between the physical world and digital world, and we are creating innovations to make this future a better place.

We work remotely from Vermont, but we very much enjoy traveling, meeting new people, and solving interesting problems.:)
